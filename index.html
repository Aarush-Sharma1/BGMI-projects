                                                                                                About BGMI

Battlegrounds Mobile India (BGMI), previously known as PUBG Mobile India is a version of PUBG Mobile, exclusively available in India. It is an online multiplayer battle royale game by Krafton. The game was initially released on 2 July 2021 for Android devices, and on 18 August for iOS devices. As of July 2022, BGMI surpassed 151 million downloads on Google Play store.The game was banned in India on July 28, 2022, by the Indian government. According to a statement from Krafton, the ban was removed on May 26,2023.

Gameplay:-

Battlegrounds Mobile India, or BGMI, is a TPP-FPP survival shooter game in which up to 100 players compete in a battle royale, a type of large-scale last man standing deathmatch in which players compete to be the last one standing. Players can enter the match as individuals or as small groups of up to four. Each match starts with players parachuting from a plane onto one of the following seven maps Erangel (Themed / Normal) Miramar Vikendi Livik (Themed / Normal) Karakin Sanhok Nusa Each round, the plane's flight path across the map changes, requiring players to quickly determine the best time to eject and parachute to the ground. Players begin with no equipment other than customised clothing options that have no effect on gameplay. Once on the ground, players can search buildings, ghost towns, and other locations for weapons, vehicles, armour, and other items. At the start of a match, these items are procedurally distributed throughout the map, with higher-risk zones typically having better equipment. Finished players can also be looted for their gear. Players can choose to play in first-person or third-person, with each having advantages and disadvantages in combat and situational awareness.

FORMAT of BGIS:-

  1. Round 1: May 2nd-5th,2024
    • 1024 Teams from in-game qualifiers.
    • Teams divided into 64 groups with 16 squads in each group.
    • Top 7 teams from each group + Top 32 among the remaining teams (Total 480 teams) qualify for Round 2.
  2. Round 2: May 9th-12th,2024
    • 512 Teams, 4 match days.
      • 480 teams from Round 1.
      • 32 teams (33rd to 64th ranked) from The Grind
    • Teams are divided into 32 groups with 16 squads in each group
    • Top 7 teams from each group + Top 16 among the remaining teams (Total 240 teams) qualify for Round 3.
  3. Round 3: May 16th-19th,2024
    • 256 Teams, 4 match days.
      • 240 teams from Round 2.
      • 16 teams (17th-32nd ranked) from The Grind.
    • Teams are divided into 16 groups with 16 squads in each group.
    • Top 3 teams from each group (Total 48 teams) qualify for Round 4
    • Top 16 teams among the remaining teams advance to Wildcard Entry
  4. Round 4: May 23rd-26th,2024
    • 64 Teams, 4 match days
      • 48 teams from Round 3.
      • 16 teams (1st-16th ranked) from The Grind.
    • Teams will be divided into 4 groups.
    • Top 4 teams from each group (Total 16 teams) advance to Semi Finals Week 1.
    • Remaining 48 teams advance to Wildcard Entry
  5. Wildcard Entry: May 30th-June 2nd,2024
    • 64 Teams, 4 match days
      • 16 teams from Round 3.
      • 48 teams from Round 4.
    • Teams are divided equally into 4 groups of 16.
    • Top 16 teams based on the cumulative overall standings advance to the Semi Finals Week 1.
  6. Semi Finals Week 1: June 6th-9th
    • 32 teams, 4 match days
      • 16 teams from Round 4.
      • 16 teams from Wildcard Entry.
    • Teams are divided equally into 4 groups of 8 teams
    • Round-robin format.
    • Top 8 teams advance to the Grand Finals
    • Remaining 24 teams proceed to Semi Finals Week 2.
  7. Semi Finals Week 2: June 13th-16th,2024
    • 24 teams, 4 match days.
    • Teams are divided equally into 3 groups of 8.
    • Double Round-robin format.
    • Top 8 teams advance to the Grand Finals.
  8. The Grand Finals: June 28th-30th at INDIA
    • 16 teams, 3 matchdays
      • 8 teams from Semi Finals Week 1.
      • 8 teams from Semi Finals Week 2.
    • 18 matches (6 each day).
